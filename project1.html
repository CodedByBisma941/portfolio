High-Converting E-commerce
Platform Redesign

Strategy-driven front-end overhaul for 15% increase in 'Add to Cart' conversions.

The Challenge & My Solution

The client's existing e-commerce interface suffered from a cluttered product page and a confusing checkout process, leading to high cart abandonment. My solution involved a complete BEM-based component restructuring** of the front-end to streamline the user flow and improve visual hierarchy, focusing entirely on Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO).

Key Results & Achievements

  • Conversion Lift:Implemented visual changes that boosted click-through rate (CTR) on primary Call-to-Action (CTA) buttons, resulting in 15% higher 'Add to Cart' rates.
  • Web Performance:Achieved lightning-fast load times under 1 second by optimizing all images and prioritizing critical CSS.
  • Accessibility: Ensured full keyboard navigation support and ARIA label implementation for strict WCAG AA compliance.
